What we do

Clinical care depends on communication, yet the quality of interaction between clinicians and patients is rarely measured systematically.

CCQ builds infrastructure to analyze clinical encounters and extract signals related to interaction and decision processes—using transcripts generated by existing ambient documentation technologies.

Our Methodology

We focus on measurement, not judgment. Our work is grounded in established decision-science and clinical communication literature and is designed to be:

  • Encounter-level and scalable
  • Vendor-agnostic and workflow-aware
  • Suitable for quality improvement and system-level analysis
Note: CCQ focuses on measurement and analysis and does not provide clinical recommendations or decision support.
